Portfolio Website

Objective:

Building a portfolio website for an abstract painter to help her reach wider audients by sharing her work online.
I put the main focus on assuring that the web content would respond and adjust when the user switches from their laptop to iPad (RWD).

Process:

Keeping that in mind I designed a website – I provided the color palette, the logo (Adobe Illustrator), the webpage layout, and the pictures (edited in Adobe Lightroom Classic). Next months included coding the separate pages, consulting with the client, and making sure the website looks good on the screens of desktop computers as well as mobile devices.

Excercises

Whenever I learned a new technology I put my newly acquired knowledge to test; Various smaller excercises allowed me to get familiar with new technoloogies by fulfilling the user stories.
